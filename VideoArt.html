Superposition

This video was for a general 3D art class. I had been interested in signal hijackings such as the Max Headroom incident and the Southern Broadcast broadcast interuption.

The software for the video was Adobe Premier Pro and the audio for the software was Audacity. The video was orginally created on the computer then recorded onto a vhs tape.

Then on the day of showing I set up a old television and a vcr and left the tape just barely in the player, just enough for it not to play.

Then something awesome (and unplanned) happened. While I was out of the room looking at another piece people had pushed the tape in and began to watch it. I was pleased with this because it made it more interactive and the audience was throughly caught off guard.
